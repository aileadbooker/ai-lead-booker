Quick start guide to automating your sales.

1. Connect Your Email

To enable the AI to read replies and update lead status, you must connect your Gmail:

  1. Go to Settings > Email Connection.
  2. Generate a Google App Password (see instructions there).
  3. Paste the password and click Save.

2. Configure Your Pitch

Go to the Settings tab to customize the emails your AI will send. You can define:

  • Initial Pitch: The first cold email sent to leads.
  • Yes Response: How to reply when they show interest.
  • No Response: How to handle objections.

3. Launch a Campaign

Go to the Campaigns tab.

  1. Enter a target niche (e.g., "Real Estate Agents in Miami").
  2. Set a daily sending limit (start with 50).
  3. Click Start Campaign.

The system will automatically find leads and start emailing them based on your settings.

4. Manage Leads

Check the Leads tab to see progress.

  • New: Freshly imported leads.
  • Qualifying: AI is analyzing them.
  • Ready to Book: They replied positively!

Click View on any lead to see the full email history and manually intervene if needed.
