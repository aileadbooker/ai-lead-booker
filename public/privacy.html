Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use AI Lead Booker, we collect information you provide directly to us (such as your name, email address, and Google OAuth credentials required to send emails on your behalf). We also collect data regarding the leads generated and emails sent through the use of our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide the AI Lead Booker service. This includes managing your account, executing email campaigns, processing payments, and providing customer support. We specifically use your Google OAuth tokens only to send outreach emails and read replies within the context of the campaigns you configure.

3. Google User Data

Our app's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use your Google data for targeted advertising, nor do we sell it to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and API tokens. All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ai.leadbooker@gmail.com.
